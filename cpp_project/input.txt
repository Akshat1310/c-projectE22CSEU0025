In the backdrop of the information age, where data proliferation is a defining characteristic, the importance of effective 
data management cannot be overstated. The ubiquity of digital information, 
ranging from text documents to multimedia files, necessitates innovative solutions for storage, transmission, and archiving. 
In this context, the Huffman Compression Tool emerges as a response to the challenges posed by the sheer volume of data generated and exchanged daily.
